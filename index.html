I'm a associate professor (CDOC) at the University of Zaragoza. I graduated in MEng Industrial Engineering, in MSc Systems Engineering and Computer Science and the Ph.D degree in Computer Science at the University of Zaragoza.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning and robotics. Most of my research is about omnidirectional cameras taking into account the combination of deep-learning with geometry. I'am also currently working in simulated prosthetic vision for improving the design of visual prostheses.
